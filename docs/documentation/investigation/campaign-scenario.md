# Feature Spec: The Campaign Scenario

## 1. The Inter-Night Sequence
At the start of every shift, the player stares at the corkboard in the cramped back office. The sequence is always:
1. **The Ledger:** Shows last night's Cash and Prestige (Star Rating). This calculates your current "Path" (Michelin, Underworld, or Viral).
2. **The City Newspaper (*L'Observateur*):** A ripped clipping with a headline reacting to your previous shift.
3. **Monsieur V.'s Memo:** A typed note from the Owner, starting with a cynical quote, followed by the actual gameplay rules for the night.

---

## 2. The 7-Night Comprehensive Scenario

### Night 1: The Grand Re-Opening (Baseline)
The tutorial night. The player learns the basic Master Clock, Queue, and Floorplan mechanics.
* **Newspaper:** *"LE SOLSTICE REOPENS: CAN IT SURVIVE THE HYPE?"*
* **Monsieur V.'s Quote:** *"A restaurant is a theater, and you are the bouncer. Don't let the ugly people on the stage."*
* **The Gameplay:** Normal queue. Introduction of **The Phantom Eater** on the Banned list. The player learns to use the Banned Tab to click his chipped gold tooth.

### Night 2: The First Temptation (The Fork in the Road)
The game introduces mechanics that force the player to choose their playstyle.
* **Newspaper:** *"NEW MAÎTRE D' SHOWS PROMISE, BUT THE REAL TEST BEGINS."*
* **Monsieur V.'s Quote:** *"Rules are the foundation of society. But cash is the foundation of this building. Don't confuse the two."*
* **The Gameplay:** **Mr. Feast** (Viral) and a low-level **Syndicate** member (Underworld) appear. They both break rules (no reservation). If the player turns them away to maintain the "vibe" (Prestige), they lock into the Michelin path. If they accept the bribes/tips, they pivot to Underworld/Viral.

---
### Nights 3 to 6: The Branching Paths
*From here, the game reads the player's Stats. The Newspaper and Memo dynamically change to match the active Path.*

#### Path A: The Underworld (High Cash, Low Prestige)
The player has been packing the grid tightly and accepting rulebreakers for cash.
* **Night 3 Newspaper:** *"LOUD NOISES AND FAT TIPS AT LOCAL BISTRO."*
* **Night 3 Memo:** *"I don't care who these men in the pinstripe suits are, as long as their money is green. Keep them happy."*
* **Gameplay Changes:** **The Syndicate** spawns frequently. You are forced to seat large parties of mobsters who bypass the queue. Honest guests get angry.
* **Night 5 Newspaper:** *"RUMORS OF MONEY LAUNDERING AT LE SOLSTICE."*
* **Night 5 Gameplay:** **Chef Balzac** attempts to sabotage your restaurant by sending fake Syndicate members to cause fights. You must use the Banned list to spot his disguise.

#### Path B: The Michelin Dream (High Prestige, Strict Rules)
The player enforces the Dress Code, rejects bribes, and keeps tables spaced out.
* **Night 3 Newspaper:** *"IS LE SOLSTICE THE STRICTEST RESTAURANT IN THE WORLD?"*
* **Night 3 Memo:** *"I smell a Michelin inspector in the city. Tighten your tie. If anyone is late, throw them into the street."*
* **Gameplay Changes:** **Gordon Angry** visits to test the menu. Time Crimes are punished severely. **Old Money Aristocrats** arrive late with glowing poodles, forcing you to hold empty tables on the grid.
* **Night 5 Newspaper:** *"CULINARY ELITE FLOCK TO IMPOSSIBLE-TO-BOOK RESTAURANT."*
* **Night 5 Gameplay:** **President Manu Macaroon** arrives. You must manage his security detail while maintaining absolute silence for the critics.

#### Path C: The Viral Circus (High Traffic, Chaos)
The player processes the queue incredibly fast but ignores the VIP rules.
* **Night 3 Newspaper:** *"THE PLACE TO BE SEEN: LE SOLSTICE TRENDING GLOBALLY."*
* **Night 3 Memo:** *"We are trending. Whatever that means. Just keep the camera flashes away from my eyes."*
* **Gameplay Changes:** **Sodium Bae** and **The Karcrashians** arrive. Flash mobs replace normal walk-ins, dropping massive 6-block Tetris shapes onto your queue that must be seated instantly.
* **Night 5 Newspaper:** *"INFLUENCER BRAWL OVER THE LAST WAGYU STEAK."*
* **Night 5 Gameplay:** **Donny Tromp** demands the biggest table while Influencers try to bypass his Secret Service. Pure chaos.

---
### Night 7: The Finale
The climax of the campaign, featuring a unique, terrifying challenge based on your path.

* **The Underworld Finale ("The Raid"):**
  * *Memo:* *"The Police are here. The Bosses are here. Do not let them sit next to each other. God help us."*
  * *Gameplay:* You must actively seat Syndicate members on one side of the grid, and Undercover Cops on the other. If their Tetris blocks touch, the restaurant is raided (Game Over).

* **The Michelin Finale ("The Three Stars"):**
  * *Memo:* *"The Head Inspector is in the queue. Perfection is not an expectation; it is a threat."*
  * *Gameplay:* The Master Clock moves twice as fast. Patience drains instantly. A single False Accusation, or letting a Banned person in, results in an immediate loss of all stars.

* **The Viral Finale ("The Live Stream"):**
  * *Memo:* *"I am going to my office to drink. Try not to burn the building down for a TikTok."*
  * *Gameplay:* The queue never stops spawning. You must seat 100 people in a single shift to win, completely ignoring standard restaurant rules to just cycle bodies through the tables.
